## Every Game Mode (What each one has)

### Core Modes
- **Solo** — Endless waves.
- **Boss** — Boss-focused fights.
- **Ranked** — 7-wave ranked challenge.
- **You vs Me** — 1v1 AI duel.
- **School** — Elemental power gameplay.
- **Survival** — Wave-based endurance.
- **Zombie** — Horde-focused survival.
- **Arena** — First to 25 kills.

### Objective / Team-Style Modes
- **Payload** — Escort cargo objective.
- **Infection** — Survive infected pressure.
- **CTF (Capture the Flag)** — Capture 3 flags.
- **King of the Hill** — Hold zone control.
- **Gun Game** — Weapon cycling progression.
- **Protect VIP** — Escort/defend VIP unit.
- **Last Man Standing** — No respawns.
- **Demolition** — Plant bomb objective.
- **Medic** — Ally rescue/support focus.

### Challenge / Variant Modes
- **Sniper Elite** — Sniper-centric combat.
- **Tag** — "Don’t be IT" survival rule.
- **Bounty** — Hunt marked targets.
- **Dodgeball** — Dodge + throw loop.
- **Blitz Rush** — Fast short rounds.
- **Juggernaut** — Tanky survival setup.
- **Stealth Ops** — High-risk stealth pressure.
- **Mirror Match** — Mirrored controls.
- **Low Gravity** — Floaty movement physics.
- **Chaos** — Randomized unpredictable waves.
- **Headhunter** — Precision/headshot emphasis.
- **Vampire** — Health drain/lifesteal style.
- **Frostbite** — Slow/control-focused pacing.
- **Titan Arena** — Bigger units + bigger hits.
- **Quick Play** — Team-vs-team bots.

## Extra Useful Info

### Typical Weapon Roles
- **Close Range:** Shotgun, Sword, Knife, Katana, Flamethrower
- **Mid Range:** Rifle, SMG, Minigun, Plasma Rifle, Dual Pistols
- **Long Range:** Sniper, Railgun, Crossbow, Harpoon Gun
- **Burst/Explosive:** RPG, Grenade Launcher
- **Control/Utility:** Freeze Ray, Boomerang, Whip

### Beginner Loadout Ideas
- **Safe starter:** Pistol + Rifle + Shotgun
- **High skill:** Sniper + Railgun + Knife
- **Crowd clear:** Flamethrower + Minigun + RPG
- **Objective push:** SMG + Shotgun + Sword

### Practical Tips
1. Reload between waves/objective windows instead of during duels.
2. Use high spread weapons in tight areas; precision weapons in lanes.
3. Save heavy ammo weapons (RPG/Railgun/Grenade Launcher) for elite threats.
4. In objective modes, prioritize map position over raw kill chasing.
5. In survival-style modes, kite enemies and avoid being cornered.
